andre.science

Welcome to andre.science! My name is Andre Mello, and the goal of this site is to share my passion for ML, math, and coding. It will feature projects and what I'm learning. I hope you will get to learn something new from here. I've benefitted so much from other people's projects, their information, and their good will. I think it's time for me to start sharing what I find worthwhile as well. One of the reasons for starting this was the push by Jeremy Howward for personal blogging while going through his fast.ai course.

About Andre

I'm a Physics student at the University of British Columbia who graduates in December. I've always been passionate about Science and knew I wanted to work in STEM ever since I can remember. Initially, I wanted to pursue physics, but while doing my bachelors I was introduced to computer science and fell in love. I realized what I really loved about physics was the math, and computer science is actually closer to that passion. It was also great to see how much more directly practical it all was. Unfortunately, I only made the hard decision to change carreers after I was very close to finishing my degree, so I chose to finish in physics, but pursue a career in computer science. Both fields share quite a lot in technical thinking and skillset, so I believe I can make the transition well.
